Partnerships

We’ve partnered with local small businesses that have a healthy, mindful, mindset, just like us! We are proud to have them on board and to offer their unique and incredible products to our customers.

Hearti Grub

Heartigrub products are made with clean, superfood ingredients to help you feel and be your best self! Made with love and integrity, their products have no refined sugars, high in protein and natural fats, but most importantly taste delicious & 'hearti'.

They come in Dairy free, Gluten-free, Keto and Vegan options too!

Hayawiiacom

Hayawiiacom offer UAE homegrown food brands. They sell eco-conscious food with a zero-waste policy. Buy their pulses & pastas, snacks, seasoning & spices, flours, honey, spreads & sauces from us today!Gluten Free, High Fibre, High Protein, Low Carb, Organic and Vegan options available.

Kind Lyfe

Kindlyfe was started by three siblings in their home! Their delicious homemade recipes are made for individuals who may struggle with some intolerances. These gut friendly, delicious snacks don't compromise on taste or texture, try them on our App today!

JuJu Scoops

juju.scoops JuJu Scoops Ice Creams are an exotic blend of fruits, spices and herbs, handcrafted with natural ingredients. Freshly made in the UAE. They offer a premium range of ice creams, including unique vegan (plant-based) flavours!Natural Ingredients, Fruits and Exotic Spices, Premium and Vegan Options. Vegan, Gluten free, refined sugar free, Preservative free, and Non-GMO.
